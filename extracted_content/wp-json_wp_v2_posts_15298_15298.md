    What is a survey funnel?\<\\/h2\>\<\\/div\>\\n\\n\\n\\nA survey funnel is the process of playing one or more surveys at key touchpoints throughout the customer journey.\<\\/p\>\<\\/div\>\\n\\n\\n\\nThese surveys have one goal, to segment your audience based on the feedback given for the purpose of delivering the most relevant product, service or content.\<\\/p\>\<\\/div\>\\n\\n\\n\\nThey can help increase conversions and identify qualified leads in all [customer\-centric activities\<\\/a\>, from email marketing to sales.\<\\/p\>\<\\/div\>\\n\\n\\n\\nIf we consider the [RACE model\<\\/a\>, survey funnels would be part of the Act stage. Where we create content for leads to interact with, in hoping to move them into the conversion stage.\<\\/p\>\<\\/div\>\\n\\n\\n\\n

    Difference between a survey funnel and a lead magnet\<\\/h3\>\<\\/div\>\\n\\n\\n\\nThere’s a fair amount of conflicting information on survey funnels, which we believe creates more confusion than is necessary.\<\\/p\>\<\\/div\>\\n\\n\\n\\nSo we’d like to clear the air for you.\<\\/p\>\<\\/div\>\\n\\n\\n\\nThe job of a survey funnel is to capture information from leads and website visitors in order to direct them to content that is most relevant to them (personalization).\<\\/p\>\<\\/div\>\\n\\n\\n\\n[Lead magnets\<\\/a\> are useful pieces of content, created with the intention of capturing lead information in exchange for access to that content.\<\\/p\>\<\\/div\>\\n\\n\\n\\nThe difference here is in lead intent.\<\\/p\>\<\\/div\>\\n\\n\\n\\nLead magnets are only capturing leads who have a specific interest in the magnet content itself. They can be effective because they’re targeted, but there’s also a natural limit to how many leads they can generate.\<\\/p\>\<\\/div\>\\n\\n\\n\\n](\"https:\/\/wp-stagesh.bugfree.co.uk\/email-marketing\/lead-magnets-and-how-they-can-grow-your-email-list\/\")](\"https:\/\/wp-stagesh.bugfree.co.uk\/digital-marketing\/race-model-for-digital-marketing\/\")](\"https:\/\/www.superoffice.com\/blog\/how-to-create-a-customer-centric-strategy\/\")
