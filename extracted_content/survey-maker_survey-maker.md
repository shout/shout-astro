Everything you need to make surveys
-----------------------------------

Whether you’re collecting customer feedback or measuring employee satisfaction, Shout has all the question types you’ll need to build surveys quickly and easily.

### NPS

Measure customer satisfaction and calculate your Net Promoter Score.

### Multiple choice

### Scales \& sliders

Gauge opinions and attitudes along a numbered scale or slider.

\[SVG\_REMOVED]\[SVG\_REMOVED]

### Matrix

\[SVG\_REMOVED]\[SVG\_REMOVED]

### Numeric

Collect numeric values as integers, decimals, currency, and respondent age.

\[SVG\_REMOVED]\[SVG\_REMOVED]

### Contact form

A pre\-built contact for collecting contact information from respondents.

\[SVG\_REMOVED]\[SVG\_REMOVED]

### Drop down lists

Save space when you have more answer options with drop down lists.

\[SVG\_REMOVED]\[SVG\_REMOVED]

### Text boxes

Collect open feedback from respondents with validated text fields.

\[SVG\_REMOVED]\[SVG\_REMOVED]

### Star ratings

Ask respondents to evaluate a statement or provide a rating along a scale of stars.
