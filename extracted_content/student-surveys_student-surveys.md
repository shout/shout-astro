Student Surveys FAQs
--------------------

Still have questions about our plans and pricing for student surveys? Let’s get into those below.

**#### Can I cancel any time?

\[SVG\_REMOVED]\[SVG\_REMOVED]

\[SVG\_REMOVED]\[SVG\_REMOVED]**

Yes! You can cancel your subscription any time via your Account Details. You’ll retain the features of your plan and access to results to the end of your billing period, at which point your subscription won’t renew.

**#### Can I switch plans?

\[SVG\_REMOVED]\[SVG\_REMOVED]

\[SVG\_REMOVED]\[SVG\_REMOVED]**

Yes, we can help you upgrade your plan at any time, e.g. Monthly (Student) to Essentials (Student). We’ll also deduct some of what you’ve paid for a plan when you upgrade to a higher one.

**#### Can you help me find respondents?

\[SVG\_REMOVED]\[SVG\_REMOVED]

\[SVG\_REMOVED]\[SVG\_REMOVED]**

Of course! You can use [Shout Audience](https://shout.com/audience/) to find survey respondents all over the world. The price of an audience will be calculated in real time as you set your preferences.

Once your survey has been approved, responses will be collected very quickly.

**#### How do I find the student plans once I create an account?

\[SVG\_REMOVED]\[SVG\_REMOVED]

\[SVG\_REMOVED]\[SVG\_REMOVED]**

When you create an account, you’ll be placed on our 14\-day free trial of the Pro plan. This will allow you to test any features you might want to use in your projects. Once you’re ready to go, click Upgrade Now in the top right of your account and you’ll be taken to our plans.

Our Student Plans can be found below the prices for our standard plans. Simply click the price for the student plan and you’ll be taken through the checkout process.
