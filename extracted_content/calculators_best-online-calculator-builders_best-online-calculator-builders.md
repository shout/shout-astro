1\. Shout.com
-------------

<img src='https://shout.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/Best-Online-Calcuator-Builders-Shout.png' alt='Best Online Calculator Builders Shout.com' title='' width='650' height='378' />

[Shout](https://shout.com/) has introduced a state\-of\-the\-art online calculator builder that places no limitations on impressions or submissions. Generate leads, [quote prices](https://shout.com/calculators/build-a-price-quote-calculator/), and engage your audience without worrying about arbitrary limitations.

Shout’s software is completely code\-free, meaning you don’t have to be a developer to create [interactive calculators](https://shout.com/development/interactive-calculator-builder/).

You can also collect feedback with surveys, collect leads with forms, and perform assessments with quizzes.

### Features

**WYSIWYG editor**: Watch your calculator come to life in real\-time, giving you a glimpse of participants’ perspective as you create.

**Import your own datasets**: Reference preexisting data in your formulas and use them to influence calculations with an [Excel\-style table](https://shout.com/help/article/337-excel-table-formula-builder).

**Custom scoring**: Assign positive and negative values to answer options to be factored into final calculations.

**Basic and advanced operators**: Use basic math operators like \+, \-, \*, / and () to build formulas as well as more advanced functions like IF statements, sin, cos and tan.

**Conditional messages**: Create personalized messages to trigger when conditional parameters are met.

**Embeds \& popups**: Embed calculators on your website or have them popup or slide out at the click of a button.

**Email gating**: Require contact information, such as names and email addresses, before showing results.

**Multi\-step calculators**: Split your fields across multiple pages to create a step\-by\-step calculator.

**Integrated CRM**: Save lead details directly to [Shout’s CRM](https://shout.com/crm/), collect consent, and group contacts based on your lawful basis for processing data.

**Brand control**: Add your logo, customize styles, remove Shout branding, and use a custom domain \& link.

**Redirect on submission**: Redirect participants to a website or landing page upon submission.

**Collaboration**: Share calculators with your [Shout team](https://shout.com/teams/) and collaborate on building and data analysis.
